Product Description

The 2021 reissue of the Air Jordan 5 Retro ‘Raging Bull’ brings back a beloved colorway originally released as one-half of 2009’s Air Jordan 5 Raging Bulls Pack. The shoe’s defining feature is a plush Varsity Red suede upper, equipped with black eyelets and a Jumpman-branded reflective silver tongue. The heel overlay displays a second Jumpman logo and an embroidered 23 on the lateral side.
